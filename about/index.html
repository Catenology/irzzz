Hi, I am Irene Zhu, a designer who is passionate about design and technology.

My specialized field is User Experience design and interaction design. I believe the most important design question to tackle is "how it should work". I am good at quickly turning concept/complex business model into wireframes and interactive prototype. Axure, Sketch and Adobe Illustrator are the primary tools I use.

In addition to the design skill, I also enjoy working cross-functionally to "figure things out", including understanding requirements, communicating design ideas, negotiating and refining features.

I am located in Seattle, WA and actively seeking out ways of making a dent to the universe.

Hobbies: Painting, traveling, hiking, photography, Miyazaki movies, Radiolab, non-fiction books, Final Fantasy XIV.
