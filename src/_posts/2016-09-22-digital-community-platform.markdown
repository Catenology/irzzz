### My role

UX Designer

### Goal
- To encourage partnership among grantees, teachers, universities
- To share best practices, lessons learned etc.
- To improve communication between teams

### Process
1. Interview and focus groups
2. Persona
3. Journey map
4. Focus group
5. Wireframe
6. User test
7. Hi-fidelity UI
