Hi, I am Irene, a designer who is passionate about design and technology. I believe design is an evolving process. Whether it's evolving from mannual to digital, or from an old version to a modern look and feel, it needs to be built upon knowledge of the current situation. That's why I like to spend time understanding my customers before drawing anything on paper. I am good at quickly turning concept/complex business model into interactive format by tackling "how it should work" first. I also enjoy working cross-functionally and learn from others.
